Cheesy Meat Skillet

Simple and hearty dish, this is basicly a rice pilaf with meat, veggies, and cheese of your choice. You can substitute just about anything here to change the flavor up.


Ingredients

  • 1/2 Yellow Onion
  • 4 cloves of Garlic
  • 2 tbsp of Butter
  • 1 cup of Jasmine Rice
  • 1 lb of Raw Meat
  • 1/2 lb of Veggies
  • 3 cups of Beef Broth
  • 4 ounces of cheese
  • 1/2 tsp of thyme
  • 1/4 tsp of sage
  • 1/2 tsp of oregano
  • 1/4 tsp of rosemary

Steps

  1. Dice the onion, mince the garlic, then saute them both in butter over medium heat till the onions are soft and translucent.
  2. While that's cooking dice the meat into small 1/2 inch pieces. Add the meat to the skillet and cook it till it's opaque on both sides.
    (The meat will finish cooking during the rest of this)
  3. Add the rice and all the spices, as well as a salt and pepper to your taste. Add the Broth to the skillet and stir to combine it all.
  4. Place a lid on the skillet, turn the heat up to medium-high, and allow the broth to come to a boil. Once it reaches a full boil,
    turn the heat down to low and let the skillet simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally, replacing the lid each time. Cooking the
    rice in an excess of broth and stirring occasionally as it cooks will produce a saucy rice mixture.
  5. After 15 minutes, the rice should be tender and saucy (if not yet tender, let it continue to cook for 5 more minutes). Stir in the veggies,
    replace the lid, and let it sit over medium-low heat for another 8 minutes to heat the vegetables through.
  6. Open the lid, add the cheese to the top, then replace the lid. Keep the lid on untill the cheese starts to melt(about 3 minutes)
